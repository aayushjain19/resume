About Me

A web designer creates visually appealing and user-friendly websites by combining graphic design with technical skills. They use tools like HTML/CSS, design software, and collaborate with clients and teams. Key traits include creativity, problem-solving, and staying updated on design trends. Web designers need a strong portfolio, time management skills, and awareness of accessibility and SEO principles.
